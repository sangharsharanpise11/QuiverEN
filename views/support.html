Quiver Support

HOW TO INSTALL QUIVER

You can install Quiver Free, Pro and Plus very easily by yourself. From the moment you start downloading your chosen version, we will support you at every step. Installation usually only takes a few minutes. If it doesn’t work for any reason, just take a look at the helpful installation guide that our team has put together for you. But if anything’s still unclear, you’re welcome to get in touch with us.

THE QUIVER MANUAL

Quiver works very intuitively, so there’s a good chance that you’ll be able to get to grips with our software by yourself. Having said that, it’s a good idea to take a look at the manual before you start working with Quiver. After all, a job well begun is a job half done. Besides, it will help you to know that you’re making the most of Quiver’s countless possibilities. We wish you every success with Quiver!

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to start with Quiver?

Quiver works on all Windows computers and laptops (from Windows 7 onwards) and with all web browsers. Quiver also works with Apple via the cloud. Our mobile apps are available for iOS (Apple) and Android smartphones and tablets. You don’t need any special extra knowledge to use Quiver. It works in a very intuitive way.
With Quiver Enterprise, things aren’t not quite so simple because this version of Quiver is customised to suit different companies. You use Quiver Enterprise to protect your entire IT infrastructure. An internal IT specialist should be assigned to install it.

What does Quiver cost?

The more comprehensive your package, the higher the price of your monthly contract. But you can use Quiver for less than €7 per month.

How does Quiver work in practice?

Good news: you can just keep working in the same way that you’re used to. But from now on, you only share files via the Quiver tab in your Quiver toolbar. As soon as you install Quiver, this toolbar appears automatically above all of your Office Suite, pdf and media files. When you click on the Quiver tab, you can state exactly who is allowed to view your files, at which location and for how long. What’s more, you can also determine what recipients are allowed to do with your files. With Quiver, you therefore retain maximal control over all your data.
